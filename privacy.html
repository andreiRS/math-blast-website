Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MathBlast! ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

MathBlast! is designed with children's privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function:

  • Game progress and scores
  • Device information (OS version, device model)
  • App performance data

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Location data
  • Contact information

How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to:

  • Save game progress
  • Improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues

Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transfer or store any data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

MathBlast! complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: mathblast@surdu.de

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
